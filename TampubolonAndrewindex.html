About Me

How I Fell in Love with Jiu Jitsu

My name is Andrew Tampubolon, and one of the biggest interests in my life is jiu jitsu.

I first fell in love with jiu jitsu after watching my first jiu jitsu tournament. My best friend, Trey Fuji, was competing, and seeing him compete made me realize how exciting and powerful the sport is.

Ever since that tournament, I have loved the sport. Jiu jitsu showed me the importance of hard work, discipline, focus, and never giving up. It inspired me to learn more and become better every day.

Watching Trey compete was a moment I will always remember. That experience changed the way I looked at sports.
It made me appreciate the skill, patience, and dedication that jiu jitsu requires.

Jiu jitsu is more than just a sport to me. It is something that motivates me and helps me grow as a person.

Why I Like Jiu Jitsu

  1. It teaches discipline
  2. It builds confidence
  3. It takes hard work and patience

Things I Enjoy About the Sport

  • Watching tournaments
  • Learning new techniques
  • Seeing friends compete
